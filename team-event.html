Post nord on a Mystery hunt

Solve A Mystery was founded in 2019 in Copenhagen by Rasmus Munch. At the beginning, we were a one-person company!

Our experience is based on authentic historical cases. It is up to the group to take on the role of criminal investigators.

You will solve various tasks and clues throughout the mystery’s self-guided tour and hopefully solve them correctly, bringing the group closer to the solution. In the end it is time for the group to point out the guilty perpetrator, at this moment the group are allowed to open the last chapter named “conclusion” to discover the truth about the case.

The experience requires teamwork, communication and problem-solving skills. The experience is brought to the group through an envelope that you collect at our pick-up points in the city of your choice. The envelope contains all the information you need for your mystery.

If you are more than 4 people we recommend that you split up into groups. Now you are able to add an extra element of excitement to the experience, by competing with each other! The group that solves the mystery and collects the most points wins.
