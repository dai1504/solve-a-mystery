About The Mystery

SWIPE FOR MORE

In ‘The Murder by Christiansborg Castle' you are taken back to the year 1914. You are a criminal investigator in Copenhagen who solves murders and other crimes. Suddenly you receive a strange letter. A very important person has been reported missing and your help is required.
You are taken to Christiansborg Castle where a horrifying sight greets you: illuminated by torches, you see a severed hand!
Who owns the hand? What happened to the person? Who is the culprit?
This and much more is up to YOU ​​to find out while you, through a self-guided city walk through Copenhagen, solve the many riddles to find the culprit.
The investigation begins at Marmorbroen and ends at Christiansborg Castle Square. The investigation lasts 2 hours, and during that time you will be taken through several historical places in the city of Copenhagen.
When you have solved the mystery, you can read about the real case behind the mystery.
So bring your best true crime friends out to Copenhagen and solve the mystery of 'The Murder at Christiansborg Castle'.
A good tip! If you are a larger group, you can buy more mysteries and compete against each other. The winner is the group that solves the mystery the fastest.
Good luck. You will need it!

How does it work?

Go to the pick up point, which is a short walk from the starting point, to collect your mystery. Begin the game any time you want at the starting point indicated on the envelope.
The mystery takes place outside and is a private group activity, no third party members or additional players are involved. It is possible to play the game in larger groups, go to FAQ for more information or send an email with your request to: contact@solveamystery.net
