Crime Cruise

Crime Cruise is inspired by a true murder case.

To play the mystery, you need to assemble a team of 2-6 people. Open the envelope, and the game can begin.
If you have multiple teams competing against each other, the team that solves the mystery and earns the most points wins.

A woman is found murdered on the ship, on her way to America. As an investigator and passenger, you and your team must find out who is behind this brutal murder.
As the story unfolds, you will solve exciting tasks together and experience a touch of the past cruises.
In the end, you will get the solution, but first, you must decide: Who is the murderer, and what evidence supports your decision?
In the envelope, you will find everything you need for the investigation.
